AVIATION LAW

The attorneys at Bouhan Falligant represent commercial and general aviation clients, including airlines, aircraft manufacturers, pilots, fixed based operators and aviation insurance companies. The aviation litigation practice group is headed by Carlton E Joyce. Mr. Joyce’s experience includes more than 25 years handling jury trials and appeals in aviation defense matters nationally, regionally, and throughout the state of Georgia.

COMMERCIAL LITIGATION

Bouhan Falligant business and corporate lawyers have also helped resolved internal conflict among business owners and ease tension between management and labor. The firm has helped corporate executives work out disagreements as to the how control of a business should be divided and how profits and expenses should be shared. The firm has brought shareholder derivative suits to ensure that officers and directors look out for the best interests of a corporation. The firm has represented both employers and employees in disputes over the enforcement of non-compete clauses, compensation, and benefits.

COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE

We have extensive experience representing local, regional and national developers in the acquisition and development of office, retail, industrial and residential real estate. Our representation includes title, acquisition, financing, zoning, governmental agreements, contracting, and leasing. Our attorneys are known for their work drafting covenants and restrictions for commercial and residential developments and condominium declarations. We create and represent many home owner and condominium associations on an ongoing basis, as well as property management companies. Jomart & Partners represents numerous financial institutions in commercial loan transactions, and in general corporate and real estate matters. We also assist banks and other lenders in the management and disposal of their REO properties. As developer and lender attorneys, we have worked with our creditor’s rights attorneys to develop expertise and experience working out troubled assets and loans. As a result, we bring a broad perspective to the table that often results in practical solutions to difficult problems.

CONSTRUCTION LAW

Jomart & Partners attorneys have the expertise to handle the broad range of issues that builders, subcontractors, suppliers, and owners may encounter in construction. Our attorneys are well equipped to meet our clients’ needs in protecting their interests and managing risks. Through our firm’s multidisciplinary approach, our construction law practice group draws from the knowledge and experience of other practice areas to offer clients the most comprehensive service. Whether the matter is related to project financing, contracts, regulatory compliance, professional negligence claims or commercial litigation, our attorneys have the experience to handle it.

EDUCATION LAW

Our attorneys have provided counsel to School Boards and School Systems to comply with the constantly evolving state federal and regulatory requirements for schools in Georgia. That advice includes compliance with applicable open meetings and open records requirements and good governance practices. The firm uses a multidiscipline approach to address the complex real estate, bonding, personnel, intergovernmental agreements, and construction requirements of school systems today. Its attorneys have successfully defended School Systems in state and federal court including claims for discrimination, breach of contract, FLSA or issues as to the siting of schools.

ELDER LAW

Attorneys at Jomart & Partners are highly knowledgeable and skilled in laws to protect elderly persons. We are diligent and determined on behalf of our clients because we care. Our attorneys have handled many issues pertaining to elder law, including, protection against elder abuse, neglect, and fraud; powers of attorney; medical care directives; consumer protection; landlord/tenant needs; real estate and mortgage assistance; tax issues; and various levels of advice, counseling and advocacy of rights. Our elder law attorneys have represented seniors and their families in legal issues regarding their safety and well-being for many years.

EMPLOYMENT LAW

Jomart & Partners attorneys take a proactive approach to employment issues for our clients. Effective use of relevant employment policies that address client needs can be a first line to avoid employer-employee disputes. We provide counsel for FMLA and disability issues and required policies for employers. The use policies to prevent discrimination and a viable complaint process in many cases may allow issues to be resolved before litigation. For those cases that are truly adversarial, our attorneys have handled regulatory claims for unemployment, matters involving claims to the EEOC as well as litigation in state and federal courts involving employment discrimination, violation of restrictive covenants or contractual disputes with employees. We are experienced in workplace investigations and training management employees how to address issues arising within the modern work force. Our attorneys bring an understanding of important provisions necessary in employment contracts for executives or other professionals being brought into or being severed from a business. Through our multidisciplinary approach, we draw from the wealth of experience in other practice areas that may apply to the client’s situation and bring that knowledge to the table. We are committed to the success of our clients.

FAMILY LAW

The attorneys at Jomart & Partners handle many complex family law matters, which include matters related to divorce, child custody and child support, among others. While cases pertaining to family law often involve difficult tasks, our attorneys meet those challenges head on by combining an extensive knowledge of family law with a strong work ethic and sensitivity to the emotional needs of our clients. Our attorneys have considerable experience trying cases before both judges and juries, but we also strive to resolve cases amicably through settlement discussions and alternative dispute resolution procedures, such as mediation, whenever appropriate. Our goal is to deliver results to our clients in the most cost-effective manner possible and with the least amount of stress possible. Many people who go through issues such as divorce or a child custody dispute become frustrated because their lawyer doesn’t treat their legal concerns as seriously as they do. At Jomart & Partners, that won’t happen to you. We take our legal responsibility very seriously. We understand what is at stake. Family law involves all the things in life that are most important to you: your relationships, your children and your property. Our family law services include the following areas: divorce, alimony, child custody, visitation, legitimation and paternity, child support, prenuptial and postnuptial agreements, restraining and protective orders, grandparent visitation, third-party custody, and post-judgment issues, such as contempt actions.
