Our Firm

Jomart & Co is an international boutique law firm comprising of a team of highly-skilled lawyers with offices in Bishkek and London.​​​​​ Our firm delivers exceptional legal services in a variety of high-profile areas. We are proficient in representing and advising clients in matters of international transactional law, offering expert advice and assistance in structuring and protecting investments as well as negotiating and drafting international contracts. We are particularly competent in dispute settlement, and render services in International Commercial Arbitration and Investor-State Arbitration covering all major jurisdictions and arbitral institutions. Our firm’s professionals are fluent in numerous languages, including English, Russian, Chinese, Turkish, Kyrgyz, Kazakh and Uzbek. Our lawyers are sensitive to the unique needs and requirements of a diverse international clientele and strive to deliver the best possible service to protect our clients’ interests. We provide high-quality legal services with the utmost professionalism and respect for our clients’ values and objectives while always holding true to our core principles:

SERVICE- We strive to meet or exceed the expectations of our clients in cognizance of the immense trust placed in us. We always endeavour to add maximum value to our clients’ enterprises in a timely, responsive, and respectful manner;

INTEGRITY- We respect at all times the ethical rules by which we are governed.

CREATIVITY- We actively seek and achieve innovative solutions to the most complex business and legal problems

EXCELLENCE- We aim to be one of the most respected and sought-after providers of legal services in the market

We perform legal services for our clients either directly or through international alliances and partnerships with other lawyers and firms in jurisdictions as diverse as the European Union, the United States of America, the United Kingdom, the United Arab Emirates, Saudi Arabia, Turkey, India, the Russian Federation, China, Hong Kong, Indonesia, Malaysia, Singapore, Kazakhstan, Tajikistan, Uzbekistan and Kyrgyzstan. Our selection of lawyers and law firms for such alliances, partnerships and associations is made after a careful analysis of their reputation in their respective jurisdictions and their ability and willingness to adhere to the same standards, values and principles as us. Our professional services and those of our correspondent network are fully insured.
